# The Podcast #225 - Tim Cook vs Apple fans

Apple’s Developer conference in 2026 is happening and it’ll be the last one when Tim Cook is the CEO of Apple as he already named John Ternus as his successor. Even though Tim Cook built a 4 trillion empire as Apple Inc, one of the places I think he missed is their relationship with developers like myself, creator of Nozbe, a productivity app available on the iPhone, iPad and the Mac. [I’ve written an entire essay about this](/cook) and I’ve let the AI co-hosts comment on it. Do you agree with my position here? Enjoy!
