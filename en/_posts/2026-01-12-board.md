# Why using a physical whiteboard helps me… think!?

🖍️ I’ve had a whiteboard in my #homeoffice for years now and even though I don’t use it daily, I love having it there for those “deep thinking” sessions.

<!--More-->

![{{ page.title }}](/img/{{ page.cover }})

It’s amazing to have so much real estate to write, draw, and sometimes fool around with colored pens to help myself to think.

Recently as I was figuring out the strategy for Nozbe for 2026, going back to the “drawing board” helped me order my thoughts.

Have you had a whiteboard? Do you like thinking with it? Let me know!
