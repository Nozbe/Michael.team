## Why book instead of a smartphone?

All studies show that the glaring light of a smartphone doesn't help calm us down. Also, what we tend to read on such a device is usually short-form dopamine-enduring content and not slow reads like a book. Again, not good.

So my new routine is this:

Before I go to bed, I pick up my [iPhone Air](/air) and do my [evening journaling](/journal) on it and then put it away for the night on a [MagSafe charger](/magsafe).

Then I pull out my brand new [Kindle Paperwhite][k] which I have set up in dark mode and I read a book. When I feel I'm getting tired, I put away the thing and fall asleep.

## Why a Kindle instead of a physical book?

With Kindle there's no additional light needed. We can turn off the light in our bedroom and both me and my wife read our books on our Kindles in a dark room.
