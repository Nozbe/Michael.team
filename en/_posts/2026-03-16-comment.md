# The habit of writing down boosts your team's productivity!

📝 Why writing things down matters? 💬 Why we spend time commenting on tasks? ✅ Because just a short paragraph TODAY can save you and your team MINUTES or HOURS of pointless conversations tomorrow! Yes, seriously!

<!--More-->

![{{ page.title }}](/img/{{ page.cover }})
## 💨 Conversations move fast!

Here’s the thing: conversations move fast. People remember things differently. Decisions get lost. Questions repeat themselves. Frustration builds.

Writing things down fixes all that — and it’s not extra work:

- 📄 Everyone’s on the same page — no more “I thought you meant…”
- ⏱️ Saves time later — fewer follow-ups, fewer repeated explanations
- 🌎 Works across time zones — async communication freely travels across the globe!
- 👍 Makes decisions visible — no one has to guess what was agreed and who thought what…

✅ At Nozbe, we document everything that matters. Notes, decisions, short summaries — it all adds up. We communicate through tasks and task comments where everything is registered and findable.

⏰ We dedicate the [extra 2 minutes](/2min) to write things down, because we know we'll thank ourselves later!

The result? Less confusion, smoother projects, and a team that knows what’s going on without endless meetings.
