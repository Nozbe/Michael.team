# StarWars Day with a Smart Brick

Today is 4th of May, so *"May the 4th… be with you"* or in [Star Wars](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Star_Wars) parlance: *"May the force be with you"*. I'm celebrating this "Star Wars day" by building the [Darth Vader](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Darth_Vader)'s ship: [Tie Fighter](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/TIE_fighter) from [Lego](/lego) with a [Smart Brick](https://www.lego.com/en-us/smart-play) and it's weird and fun:

## Flying with the Smart Brick

Now that it's done, you can put the "Smart Brick" inside and it makes noises. From my understanding it's based on NFC and gyroscope, so depending on which kind of brick we put next to it, it responds. When the Darth Vader sits inside the ship, it plays the "[Imperial March](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Imperial_March)".

![{{ page.title }} fly](/img/may4-fly.jpg)

When you move the starship, it makes whooshing sounds as well, but these are pretty underwhelming.
