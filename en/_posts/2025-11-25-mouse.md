## Because [#productivity](/productivity) - it's faster this way!

When I was in high school I was working for my dad as a "designer" in Corel Draw (manipulating vector objects) and I realized that when I used the mouse with right hand, I was very slowâ€¦

Because as I move an object and edit its label, I need to take my hands off the mouse on the keyboard to type it. Slow.

However, when I switched hands, I realized I could manipulate an object with my mouse in my left hand and type with my right hand. Faster and more efficient.

So I started using a mouse with my left hand. And after a few months I was as good as with my right hand.
