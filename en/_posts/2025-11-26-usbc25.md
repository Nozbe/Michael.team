# My favorite USB-C gadgets of 2025

In one of my earlier blog posts I mentioned that now I'm striving to get [USB-C all things](/usbc), meaning whenever I get a new gadget it must be powered using just a standard USB-C cable. This way I don't collect useless additional chargers and can always add juice to my devices. Practical and environmentally friendly. Here are my top picks for this year and great ideas for gifts for nerds like me - send this list to your partners, people.

## AAA and AA USB-C Li-ion batteries

Most people don't know these things exist. But they do. And they are awesome. I'm gradually changing all my previous Ni-MH rechargeable batteries to these ones.

![{{ page.title }} aa](/img/usbc25-aaa.jpg)

Not only do they add USB-C charging, but they are also much modern using Li-ion and NOT Ni-MH technology. They come with this cute 4-in-1 USB-C charging cable and they can quickly recharged. I will only be buying these batteries from now on. Hare are links to some of the popular [AA batteries](https://amzn.to/44nVxOD) and [AAA ones](https://amzn.to/3JVon22).

## SUPRENT Beard trimmer (not Braun!)

I've been rocking a beard for the last 6 years and I've been trimming it using a Braun trimmer which I still have at home. However, for travel I bought a new trimmer which is USB-C powered and looks like a Braun knock-off (the same length dial and basic design).

![{{ page.title }} trimmer](/img/usbc25-trimmer.jpg)

To me, Braun brand will be irrelevant if they don't switch to USB-C at some point. I got the [SUPRENT beard trimmer and it's great](https://amzn.to/4oUey3n).

## HOTO screwdrivers

In my house there's always something to fix and something to screw in so now I'm using two sets of HOTO screwdrivers. One for the [bigger stuff (furniture, wall, etc)](https://amzn.to/4rj2lHg) and [one for the smaller stuff (computers and electricity)](https://amzn.to/4inIYJa).

![{{ page.title }} hoto](/img/usbc25-hoto.jpg)

They are very well made, charge via USB-C (duh!) and have all the necessary magnetic bits to screw everything in or out.

## INIU 3-in-1 travel Qi2 charger

I'm about to travel to the US for a few days and I'm taking this very compact travel charger. With this one device I can charge my [iPhone Air](/air), [AirPods Pro 3](/airpodspro) and [Apple Watch Ultra 2](/ultra).

![{{ page.title }} 3in1](/img/usbc25-3in1.jpg)

There are many compact 3-in-1 chargers on the market now, but this one has been throughly tested by one of my trusted YouTubers ([MobileReviewsEh](https://www.youtube.com/@MobileReviewsEh)) and he considers it as the best one. [Around $40 bucks on Amazon](https://amzn.to/4ieBEPK).

## AIRPODS 3 Pro headphones

The third generation of [AirPods Pro](/airpods) headphones doesn't disappoint. They are better in almost every way. Better noise cancellation, longer range when connected to my devices and quicker switching between them. I use them every day and keep them in my pants pocket at all times. I also love how convenient it is to charge them. Not only using USB-C cable, but also MagSafe and Apple Watch inductive charging. You can find them [discounted on Amazon](https://amzn.to/48ur57U).

![{{ page.title }} beats](/img/usbc25-beats.jpg)

## BEATS Solo 4 Wireless

Speaking of headphones, this one is not for me, but for my daughters. All of my girls (including my wife) have these headphones. My elder ones have Beats Solo 3 Wireless (earlier model) and my youngest will receive the latest model this Christmas. They don't have noise-canceling but as they are on-ear headphones they block enough noise from the outside. They are very sturdy and even when they break, [they can be easily fixed](/soda/). They have Apple's special chip so they easily connect to my daughters' iPhones and iPads. Perfect travel companions. They are not very cheap but very often [they are on sale on Amazon](https://amzn.to/3XeDTck) and then they're totally worth getting for the kids.

## ANKER 140W 3 x USB-C charger
