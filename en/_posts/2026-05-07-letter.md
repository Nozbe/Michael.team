# A letter from founder to a fellow entrepreneur?

How about adding such letter on main [Nozbe][n] home page? Just to show the prospective buyer that the same tool we're selling is the same tool we're using and it's reflecting our values and the way we want to work? What do you think? Would that convince you?

<!--More-->

![{{ page.title }}](/img/{{ page.cover }})

## ✍️ Dear fellow entrepreneur,

In 2007, I built the first version of Nozbe for one reason: to get myself organized. An audience of one — me.

Twenty years and hundreds of thousands of users from all over the world later, Nozbe is a company of almost 20 people and we still use Nozbe to run it as our real, daily, this-is-how-we-work tool. Thanks to it:

**We have few very meetings.** On average I have 1-3 meetings per week. Decisions happen inside tasks, in writing, where everyone can see them.

**I’m not the bottleneck.** My team doesn’t wait for me to unblock them. They see what’s happening across every project and move forward without asking permission.

**Nothing falls through.** Decisions live in tasks. Context lives in comments. My future self always finds what my past self left behind.

**I sleep better.** I don’t carry the company in my head anymore. I open Nozbe, see where things are, close the laptop. The stress of *not knowing* is gone.

**My team enjoys using it.** No training. Our tool is powerful yet simple to use, so we all feel productive.

This is the company I always wanted to run. Calm, clear, built on trust. With shared values of Passion, Simplicity, Freedom and Fairness.

## 👊 And the tool that makes it possible is the same one I’m offering you.

Try it free. Invite your team when you’re ready. See what happens when work… just works.

Yours,

**Michael Sliwinski**

Founder & CEO, Nozbe
