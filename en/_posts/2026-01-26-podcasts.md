# Podcasts I listen to and recommend

Did you know I spent 228 hours listening to podcasts in 2025? At 1.5x speed? A podcast creator (I co-host NoOffice.FM and Nadgryzieni.pl) is also a podcast consumer. Here are the podcasts I listened to the most and I recommend:

<!--More-->

![{{ page.title }}](/img/{{ page.img }})

## My favorite podcasts based on what I really listen

- [ATP - Accidental Tech Podcast](https://atp.fm) - my favorite show featuring Marco, John and Casey
- [Deep Questions with Cal Newport](https://www.thedeeplife.com/listen/) - my favorite author of “Deep Work” talks productivity. Brilliant.
- [The Talk Show with John Gruber](https://daringfireball.net/thetalkshow/) - my favorite tech blogger sometimes has amazing guests.
- [Nadgryzieni](http://nadgryzieni.pl) - I co-host this podcast (it’s in Polish) so I sometimes re-listen to some episodes after we’ve recorded - but I do recommend you subscribe if you’re Polish and you want to know what’s cool in tech.
- [Solved with Mark Manson](https://daringfireball.net/thetalkshow/) - the most over-researched podcast on the planet - amazing.
- [Rework by 37signals](https://37signals.com/podcast/) - I might [not agree](/basecamp) with everything they do, but I like listening to David and Jason.
- [Under the Radar](https://www.relay.fm/radar) - I used to love listening to David and Marco, unfortunately after 10 years they stopped recording this podcast.
- [Nathan Barry Show](https://www.youtube.com/@thenathanbarryshow) - I’m a big fan of Nathan as an entrepreneur and he invited me on his podcast and our show will air in March of this year!
- [Mała Wielka Firma](https://malawielkafirma.pl/) - my favorite business podcast in Polish by my favorite podcaster and a good friend!

These were my favorite listens of 2025…

Any podcasts you’d recommend?
