# No Office FM #93: Why Most Teams Drown in Chaos

Does your team constantly lose track of things, waste time searching for information, or spend the whole day putting out fires instead of doing meaningful work? In this episode I invited my friend and productivity expert [Augusto Pinaud](/augusto) to talk about how GTD (Getting Things Done) principles — adapted for teams — can reduce chaos, improve accountability, and help everyone stay aligned without endless meetings and nonstop messaging.
