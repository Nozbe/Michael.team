# What's in my USA trip tech pouch in 2025?

This week I'm traveling to the USA on business as I've been offered a quite unique opportunity to meet one of my business gurus - [Nathan Barry](https://nathanbarry.com) - and record a podcast with him - [the Nathan Barry Show](https://www.youtube.com/@thenathanbarryshow). It's a quick trip as I'm staying in the US for only 3 nights. And it's a long trek, as I'll be flying close to 20 hours each way. For this trip I decided to pack ultra-light and get only the most necessary tech:

## My tech pouch for a quick trip

My main computers on this trip will be the [M4 iPad Pro](/ipadm4) and [iPhone Air](/air).

![{{ page.title }} list](/img/pouch-small-list.jpg)

## Headphones for the trip

I'm taking two of them:

- **Bose QC-35 headphones** - I've had them for almost a decade now - they still work great and are prefect for long airplane flights. [Maybe I should get the newer ones?](https://amzn.to/44kIptE)
- [AirPods Pro 3](/airpods) - always have them in my pocket.

## Backpack is the Peak Design ZIP

And I'm packing everything in my favorite [Peak Design ZIP backpack](/zip), where I also carry:

- [Anker 20K 100W Power bank](https://amzn.to/4pwLzmw) with a 3-in-1 USB-C to USB-C/Micro-USB/Lightning cable. This thing is awesome - it takes 100W as well to re-charge, so it's full in a little over an hour.
- [Kindle](/kindle) - I want to keep cultivating my reading habit.
